## FAQ

### Q: How do I connect UART?
A: Use the dedicated 3-pin connector (next to HDMI) at 115200 baud, 8N1.

### Q: What recording formats are supported?
A: H.264/H.265 video with AAC audio, output in MP4/MKV containers.

### Q: Can I use this without a display?
A: Yes! Use the UART console or web dashboard.

### Q: How much storage do I need?
A: Approximately 4GB per hour of 1080p video.

### Q: What cloud providers are supported?
A: Nextcloud, OneDrive, AWS S3, and any WebDAV-compatible service.
